Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

This policy explains what personal data the Portrait Realism Academy collects, why, and the choices and rights you have. By using this website (portraitrealismacademy.com) or submitting the application form, you acknowledge this policy.

Data controller: Federico Galliani
Contact for privacy requests: federicogallianitattoo@gmail.com

1. What we collect

Information you give us. When you fill in the application form we collect the details you enter: first and last name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number (optional), Instagram handle (optional), your stated level, and anything you write in the message field.

Information collected automatically.

  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — only if you accept marketing cookies. It collects device and usage data and online identifiers so we can measure and improve our advertising. It is provided by Meta Platforms.
  • Privacy-friendly site analytics (via Cloudflare) — aggregate, cookieless statistics such as pages viewed and approximate country/city, derived from your connection. Any identifier used for counting is one-way hashed and rotated daily; we do not store your raw IP address, and this analytics does not identify you.
  • Standard server/security data (e.g. request logs) needed to deliver and protect the site.

2. Why we use it & our legal basis

  • To handle your application and contact you about the Academy (availability, deposit, details) — pre-contractual steps at your request / our legitimate interest.
  • Advertising measurement (Meta Pixel) — your consent, which you give via the cookie banner and can withdraw at any time.
  • Security, performance and anonymous statistics — our legitimate interest in running a safe, working website.

3. Cookies & your choice

We do not load the Meta Pixel until you press Accept on the cookie banner. If you decline, no marketing cookies are set. Our Cloudflare analytics uses no cookies at all. You can change your choice at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted service providers (processors) only to run the Academy:

  • Meta Platforms — advertising and pixel measurement (only after your consent).
  • FormSubmit — delivers your application form submission to our email.
  • Google — email service that receives your application.
  • Cloudflare — content delivery, security and cookieless analytics.
  • Our website hosting provider.

5. International transfers

We are based in Italy and the Academy takes place in the United States. Your data may be processed in the U.S. and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the providers' standard contractual clauses) for these transfers.

6. How long we keep it

We keep application data for as long as needed to consider and follow up on your application, and up to 24 months afterwards, then delete it — unless we are legally required to keep it longer. Aggregate, anonymous analytics may be retained without time limit as it does not identify you.

7. Your rights

If you are in the EEA or UK (GDPR): you may request access to, correction, deletion, restriction or portability of your data, object to certain processing, and withdraw consent at any time. You can also lodge a complaint with your data protection authority (in Italy, the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).

If you are in California (CCPA/CPRA): you may request to know, delete or correct your personal information, and opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" — note that we do not sell your data.

To exercise any right, email federicogallianitattoo@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by law.

8. Children

This site and the Academy are intended for professional adults and are not directed to anyone under 18.

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always dated at the top of this page.
