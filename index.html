The masterclass

Learn the black & grey portrait — and how to make it last.

Federico Galliani builds photorealistic black & grey portraits with true grey wash — black diluted with water, not grey ink — and an obsessive attention to detail. His guiding principle: a portrait is judged not by how it looks fresh, but by how it holds and ages in the skin. Over three days you'll learn the theory, watch him work, and tattoo under his direct supervision.

The program

Three days, one skill.

Day 1 · Oct 6

Theory & foundations

The portrait realism system: reference reading, values and tones, grey wash mixing, machine & needle setup, and how to build contrast that stays readable as it heals.

Day 2 · Oct 7

Live demo with Federico

Federico tattoos a full portrait step by step, explaining every decision — layering, saturation, skin tones, highlights and the details that separate a good portrait from a great one.

Day 3 · Oct 8

Your supervised practice

You tattoo your own portrait on a real model (provided by the host studio) with Federico guiding you at the machine — real-time feedback on your work.

FAQ

Good to know.

Who is this masterclass for?

Working tattoo artists at an intermediate or advanced level who want to specialize in black & grey portrait realism. It is taught in English.

How much is it and how do I reserve a seat?

Tuition is $2,000. A $500 deposit reserves one of the 10 seats; the remaining balance can be paid in installments before the class. Apply through the form below and you'll receive the deposit link.

What do I need to bring?

Only your own tattoo machine. Every student gets a full workstation, all working materials and ink supplied for the class.

Will there be real models to tattoo?

Yes. On Day 3 you tattoo a real model under Federico's supervision. Models are provided by the host studio, SOL Studios.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes — a certificate of completion is issued at the end of the three days.

What is the refund policy?

The deposit reserves your seat and is non-refundable except in cases of force majeure. If the Academy is cancelled by the organization, you receive a full refund.

Where exactly is it held?

SOL Studios, 11000 W Janesville Rd, Hales Corners, WI 53130 — in the Milwaukee area, opening the weekend of the Milwaukee Tattoo Arts Convention.
