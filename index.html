FAQ

Good to know.

Who is this masterclass for?

Working tattoo artists at an intermediate or advanced level who want to specialize in black & grey portrait realism. It is taught in English.

How much is it and how do I reserve a seat?

Tuition is $2,000. A $500 deposit reserves one of the 10 seats; the remaining balance can be paid in installments before the class. Apply through the form below and you'll receive the deposit link.

What do I need to bring?

Only your own tattoo machine. Every student gets a full workstation, all working materials and ink supplied for the class.

Will there be real models to tattoo?

Yes. On Day 3 you tattoo a real model under Federico's supervision. Models are provided by the host studio, SOL Studios.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes — a certificate of completion is issued at the end of the three days.

What is the refund policy?

The deposit reserves your seat and is non-refundable except in cases of force majeure. If the Academy is cancelled by the organization, you receive a full refund.

Where exactly is it held?

SOL Studios, 11000 W Janesville Rd, Hales Corners, WI 53130 — in the Milwaukee area, opening the weekend of the Milwaukee Tattoo Arts Convention (Oct 9–11). Many artists come for both.
