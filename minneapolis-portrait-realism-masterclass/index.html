From Minneapolis, the Portrait Realism Academy is a short nonstop flight — about 75 minutes MSP→MKE, then 15 minutes to the venue — or roughly a 5-hour drive.

Getting to the masterclass from Minneapolis

Fly MSP→MKE in about 75 minutes; Milwaukee Mitchell (MKE) is only ~10 miles / 20 minutes from SOL Studios. By car it's roughly 337 miles / about 5 hours.

Venue: SOL Studios, 11000 W Janesville Rd, Hales Corners WI 53130

Why come from Minneapolis

The Twin Cities are a strong stop on the convention circuit, and this is the closest 3-day portrait-realism masterclass to fly in for. And the masterclass opens the weekend of the Milwaukee Tattoo Arts Convention (Oct 9–11) — one trip, both.
