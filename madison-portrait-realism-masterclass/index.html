From Madison, the Portrait Realism Academy is a straight shot east — about 78 miles / an hour and twenty minutes on I-94 to Hales Corners.

Getting to the masterclass from Madison

Roughly 78 miles / 1 hour 20 minutes due east on I-94. Close enough to commute in daily or stay over for the three days.

Venue: SOL Studios, 11000 W Janesville Rd, Hales Corners WI 53130

Why come from Madison

There's no dedicated portrait-realism intensive in Madison — this is the nearest serious black & grey masterclass, with a Milwaukee-area venue. And the masterclass opens the weekend of the Milwaukee Tattoo Arts Convention (Oct 9–11) — one trip, both.
